I work round the corner from this historic cemetery (its name derives from 'bone hill'). [It was used as a burial site for nonconformists from the late seventeenth century](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bunhill_Fields) and is a lovely setting to sit quietly and have lunch.
