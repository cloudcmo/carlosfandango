_Years_ ago (like, 1994) I stumbled across a club called Chaise Longue\* in Regent Street. It was a crooner/cheesey listening night and devoid of any sneering/irony - they loved the tracks. This was before the flaccid irony of Mike Flowers Pops (though I liked him too), "I love the 70s" TV shows and Peter Kay.

I left the club with a free home-made cassette which I've played to death. Literally - this morning it won't play without wowwwwwing and slurrrrring.

So for posterity, I've re-created it\*\* on Spotify. [Crank up and enjoy](http://open.spotify.com/user/carllyons/playlist/0FNyRhJehaKPKgSX3WXcOA "Spotify").
