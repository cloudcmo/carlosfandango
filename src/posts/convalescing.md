Dylan has one more week with the plastic hat on. By and large, she's  
doing remarkably well. She's stoic and doesn't complain even though  
everything must be frustrating and bewildering.

This sunny Sunday morning is a treat for both of us. I'm reading a  
book and she's chomping at passing flies.
