I went to an exhibition a few years ago called [Fifa 100](http://www.guardian.co.uk/football/2004/mar/04/newsstory.sport15), celebrating the greatest 100 footballers of all time. There were fabulous photographic portraits of each player, showcasing them in full, beautiful flow.

Except for Gordon Banks, whose picture was so ordinary and domestic that I had to buy the mug.
