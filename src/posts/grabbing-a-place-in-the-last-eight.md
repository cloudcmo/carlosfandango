Exclusive interview with Prediction Game leader, DK Willy. I asked DKW earlier today what the secret of his success was.

> _"Firstly, it's important to have a clean space. I lock myself in the shed with a bottle of sherry, some matches dipped in wax and the Beano annual, 1976. I pin up a world cup wallchart next to a picture of Terry Butcher and then close my eyes. Chanting the words of Robert De Niro's waiting, I throw part-sucked Werther's Originals at the fixtures. When they land on a team, I mark a cross in my special book."_

So that, kids, is how WINNERS do it.
