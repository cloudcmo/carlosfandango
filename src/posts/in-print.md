[That reporter was true to his word](http://www.carlmesnerlyons.com/2010/10/trying-out-sports-photography/) and published one of my photos of the Dulwich Hamlet game in the local paper. He used my least favourite picture [from the set](http://www.flickr.com/photos/carl201167/sets/72157625099877209/show/), but I guess it was of the winning goal. Either way, I am now officially a published sports photographer ;-D
