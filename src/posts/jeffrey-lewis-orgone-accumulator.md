Went to see [Jeffrey Lewis](http://www.thejeffreylewissite.com/index.html) and Peter Stampfel at the Windmill, Brixton (fab venue) last night. At first I was disappointed that he didn't play any of his solo stuff, but all the songs worked great. Highlights included a poem about Pussy Riot, a very funny 'short film' (actually a comic) called After Dark, but I really, really loved a very unexpected and feedback-laden cover of [Hawkwind's Orgone Accumulator](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KhWArBhPWu0).
