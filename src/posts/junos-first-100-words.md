Here's our 19 month old's first 100 words. Roughly in order of appearance. She started to speak with any coherence in November and the last two months have been amazing. It's interesting (to us at least) that verbs, adjectives and (especially) prepositions are starting to appear -  enabling lovely little sentence fragments such as "legs hot", "daddy up" and "boobies out" 1.
