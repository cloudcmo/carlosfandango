One of my favourite pleasures is to walk the dog through Nunhead Cemetery. Early weekend mornings are lovely and quiet, and it feels reflective and peaceful. The paths on the walk curve and climb, and at the very peak there's a bench with a direct line of sight through the trees to St Paul's some four miles away. It's hard to show this on a cameraphone picture as the exposures necessary are usually too diverse. However, it's a perfect job for the new HDR feature on the iPhone 4. I think you can just see the church through the clearing.
