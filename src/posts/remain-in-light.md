I ought to vote to Leave. In my middle age, I'm sceptical of bureaucracy and a believer in free enterprise. I love the plurality of culture in Europe but I'm wary of hitching our economies together, three-legged race style.  But I'm also afraid of rising nationalism, isolationism and blaming other people for our problems. Let's not retreat and hide bitterly indoors, it's better to contribute to our community and change things for the better.  Remain.
