It's a fairly impressive but dry day at the Oval. The crowd are  
filling the fun gap by creating huge stack towers of empty (plastic)  
beer glasses.

The entertainment comes when the stewards, duty-bound, come to get  
them, only for people to hand them off the other way,  
piggy-in-the-middle style. Much cheering ensues.

It's civil disobedience, the cricket way. I bet Boris would love it.
