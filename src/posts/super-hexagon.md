The best compliment I can pay Super Hexagon is that it's the sort of game Eugene Jarvis ([http://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eugene\_Jarvis](http://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eugene_Jarvis)) would have made had iOS been around in the age of Defender and Robotron. 

It's adrenaline fast, infuriatingly hard and compulsive with strobing vector graphics and insistent techno. It's Tempest gone rogue. 

[https://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/super-hexagon/id549027629?mt=8](https://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/super-hexagon/id549027629?mt=8)
