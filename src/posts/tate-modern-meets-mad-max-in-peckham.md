Went so see [the Bold Tendencies III art exhibition](http://londonist.com/2009/06/art_preview_rooftop_sculpture_park.php) on top of a car park in SE15. It was hard to find, the entrance (at least the one we found) being unmarked and down an alley. The 'Heaven' lift to the upper floors was particularly creaky and we wondered if sound effects had been added for art's sake or whether it was genuinely a risk to life. But once there, it was a treat. Three floors of sculpture and installation topped off with an impossibly cool Campari bar. The 360 degree views across London were terrific too.
