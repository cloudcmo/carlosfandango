Loving this word for South London _Transpontine: 'on the other (i.e. the south) side of the bridges over the Thames; pertaining to or like the lurid melodrama played in theatres there in the 19th century'._ [Via](http://transpont.blogspot.co.uk/2013/12/transpontine-word-of-day.html?m=1)
