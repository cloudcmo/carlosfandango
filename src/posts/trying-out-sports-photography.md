I decided to take my new lens down to Dulwich Hamlet this afternoon (_"c'mon you 'amlet!"_). Things I learned:

1.  Sports photography is _hard_
2.  Shots of goal kicks are not exciting
3.  Having [a long lens](http://usa.canon.com/cusa/consumer/products/cameras/ef_lens_lineup/ef_70_200mm_f_2_8l_is_ii_usm) makes people think you're a pro. I got given a business card by a reporter from the local paper asking me to send any good ones for publication(!)
